Frequently Asked Questions

  • What ingredients are in Reese's products?

    Reese's products primarily contain chocolate, peanut butter, sugar, milk, and additional ingredients for texture and flavor. Specific ingredient lists are available on the product packaging and the company's website.

  • Are Reese's products gluten-free?

    Some Reese's products are gluten-free, while others are not. Please refer to the ingredient label on the packaging or the Reese's website for detailed information on gluten content.

  • Do Reese's products contain any allergens?

    Yes, Reese's products often contain peanuts, milk, and soy. Always check the packaging for specific allergen information and warnings.

  • Where can I find Reese's products in stores?

    Reese's products are widely available in grocery stores, convenience stores, and online retailers. You can use the Product Locator on the Reese's website to find specific products near you.

  • Are there any vegan options available?

    Currently, Reese's products contain milk and are not suitable for vegans. The company is continuously exploring new options, so check the website for any updates.

  • How can I contact Reese's customer service?

    You can contact Reese's customer service via email at reeses_official@gmail.com or by phone at the number provided on the Reese's website. There is also a contact form available on the website for your convenience.

  • Can I purchase Reese's products in bulk?

    Yes, Reese's products can be purchased in bulk through various online retailers and wholesale stores. Check the Reese's website for more information and links to bulk purchasing options.

  • Does Reese's offer any seasonal or limited edition products?

    Yes, Reese's frequently offers seasonal and limited edition products, especially around holidays such as Halloween, Christmas, and Easter. Keep an eye on the Reese's website and social media channels for announcements and availability.

  • How should I store Reese's products?

    Reese's products should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For best results, keep them at room temperature or slightly cooler.

  • Can I get nutritional information for Reese's products?

    Yes, nutritional information is available on the packaging of Reese's products and on the Reese's website. This includes details on calories, fat, sugar, protein, and other nutritional values.

  • Is there a way to track my order if I purchase Reese's products online?

    Yes, if you purchase Reese's products online through the official website or authorized retailers, you will receive tracking information via email. You can also check your order status on the retailer's website.

  • Are there any Reese's products made without artificial ingredients?

    Reese's is committed to quality and transparency in its ingredients. Some products may be made without artificial ingredients. For specific details, check the product packaging or the Reese's website.
